What kanopy is

Built for how
designers actually think.

01 — Facilitate, don't generate
Your thinking, amplified
kanopy asks sharp questions that push beyond your first idea. It surfaces contradictions, tests assumptions, and names tensions — without replacing your judgment with AI output.
02 — Works at every stage
From diverge to ship
Start with a blank challenge. End with a scored, prioritized roadmap and a Jira-ready backlog. Every phase is connected — no copy-pasting between tools.
03 — Spatial, not just linear
A canvas that thinks with you
The Board watches your card density and clustering patterns, and nudges you when the right phase transition emerges. AI that knows when to speak up.

◎

◈
Synthesize — Names tensions and surfaces contradictions. Gives provocative theme labels and asks about the gap.
◇
Pressure Test — Finds the weakest assumption in a direction you're committed to. Stress-tests before you build.
