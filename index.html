Why Canopy

Most AI tools flatten your thinking into a prompt box.
Canopy gives you altitude.

When you're designing, you're inside the problem. You see the detail but miss the pattern. You feel the tension but can't name it. You have the ideas but can't see how they connect.

Canopy works as the intelligence layer underneath your process — surfacing what you can't see when you're in the weeds. It detects overarching themes, patterns across ideas, and system-level tensions before they become mistakes you ship.

"The best designers don't just solve problems — they see the system behind them. Canopy helps you do that faster and communicate it to anyone in the room."

The designer is the intelligence. Canopy is the altitude. You make every call. Canopy makes sure you've seen everything worth seeing before you do.

What Canopy actually does

Not a prompt box.
An engine.

Most AI tools answer questions. Canopy runs a complete design thinking system — seven interconnected capabilities, one continuous session.

01
🌳
Facilitates design thinking & brainstorming
Three coaching modes — Diverge, Synthesize, Pressure Test — that ask the question behind your question. Never generic. Never a list.
02
🔭
Surfaces research & patterns
Behavioral data from ContentSquare, Hotjar, and Maze woven directly into your session. Canopy names themes and surfaces what you can't see from inside the problem.
03
⚡
Challenges assumptions
Pressure Test mode finds the weakest assumption in your thinking and presses on it before you commit. The inversion question. The constraint flip.
04
◈
Generates concept directions
Canopy clusters your ideas into named themes, scores them on value and feasibility, and reveals the strategic bets hiding inside your messy brainstorm.
05
◻
Creates prototypes
Any idea in your session becomes a UI in seconds. Use your design system, or color outside the lines. Prototypes feed back into the board — nothing leaves the session.
New
06
▲
Prioritizes opportunities
Quick Wins. Strategic Bets. Table Stakes. Canopy runs the matrix — votes, value, feasibility, strategic fit — and gives you a ranked list with rationale.
07
→
Produces actionable roadmaps
Three-phase roadmaps with milestones, success metrics, and sequenced tasks. Export as slides, generate user stories for Jira, Asana, Monday, Airtable, or Linear — in one click.
