The kanopy workflow

From signal
to roadmap.

Six connected stages. Every idea flows forward. No copy-pasting between tools. Start anywhere, end with something you can actually ship.

01
Research & Competitive Analysis New
Connect ContentSquare, Analytics, and your existing tools. kanopy synthesizes behavioral data, crawls competitor experiences, and assesses your design files — surfacing the highest-priority improvements for any journey. Generate "bold ideas" that push industry benchmarks, not just match them.
02
Think — AI Facilitation
Three modes. Diverge asks extreme questions — analogies, inversions, constraint-flips. Synthesize names tensions and contradictions. Pressure Test finds the weakest assumption in a direction you're already committed to. Capture anything worth keeping, send to Board in one click.
03
Board — Ideate & Cluster
A spatial canvas that watches your patterns. Cards accumulate, clusters emerge, and the AI nudge bar activates when it's time to move forward. Vote, drag, color-code by author. The Board thinks with you.
04
Score — Value × Feasibility
AI evaluates every idea on value and feasibility. Quick Win, Strategic Bet, Table Stakes, Deprioritize. Four quadrants, no committee, re-run anytime. The 2×2 that actually moves work forward.
05
Prototype — Quick Wireframe
Any message or card becomes a conceptual screen in one click. Choose Figma, Material 3, Apple HIG, Freeform, or your own design system. 16 component types, 4 screen sizes. Not a final design — a thinking tool.
06
Export — Ship it
Three-phase roadmap with milestones and success metrics. Download as a slide deck, Markdown, or user stories formatted for Jira, Linear, Asana, Notion, Airtable, or Monday. From insight to backlog without copy-paste.
